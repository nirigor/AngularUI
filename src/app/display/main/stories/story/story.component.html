The moving company failed to disclose the damage upfront, compelling you to order expensive replacement parts to attempt to repair the piano as much as possible. Despite your efforts, a significant gap will persist, as the new parts can never precisely replace what was lost.

This situation represents a devastating blow for you as a professional musician, as the sound and qualities of the piano have been irreversibly compromised.
