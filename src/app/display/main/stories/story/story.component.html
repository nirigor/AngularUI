Neighbors

Last year the opposite neighbor used the rooftop as a temporary guesthouse without the consent of the other tenants. You voted against the neighbor's plan, claiming the rooftop should remain the common area. The neighbor quickly replied, “you will regret this”. The next morning the opposite neighbour sent a message to the building's WhatsApp group saying, “they don't care about the welfare of babies; they only care about themselves”. You were hurt by this message since you have nothing against babies and are happy to get along with the other tenants. You tried to explain to the opposite neighbour that it has nothing to do with the baby; it's just the fact you believe the common area should stay common. The neighbour ignored your calls and hung up a poster regarding your vote in the building's lobby, damaging your good name. You tried to ask the neighbour to stop spreading false lies about you, but he denied your request.

You want to maintain a good relationship with the other tenants. You struggle to understand why the opposite neighbor won't cooperate, and you would like a civilized relationship between you. You would like the neighbor to stop spreading lies and apologize for the posters and false messages he wrote.

You're disappointed with the bank for not telling you that the insurance policy needs to be updated when the loan terms are changed, and you're disappointed with the insurance company and its insensitive answers.
