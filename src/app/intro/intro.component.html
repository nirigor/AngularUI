Terms of use

What is the purpose of this research?

The purpose of this research is to study litigants' needs, interests and emotions when facing a legal conflict.
Participation is voluntary. It is your choice whether or not to participate in this research. If you choose to participate, you may change your mind and leave the study at any time.

NOTE: We have implemented measures to ensure truthful and thoughtful participation... failing to adhere to these measures would result in immediate disqualification.
A reward token will be granted only upon successful completion of the survey.

What are the risks and possible discomforts?

If you choose to participate, there are no known risks associated with your participation in this research beyond those of everyday life.

If I take part in this research, how will my privacy be protected?

The data we collect will be saved on a secured server. Diligent care will be taken to protect sensitive information. The information will be used solely for the purposes of this research. No identifiable information will be saved.

If I have any questions, concerns or complaints about this research study, who can I talk to?

The leading researcher for this study is Dr. Avshalom Elmalech who can be reached at yigal.nirenberg@live.biu.ac.il.
